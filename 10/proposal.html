Lake House Rentals - Site Plan

Berick Smith

WDD 130-16

Overview

Purpose

The primary purpose of the Lake House Vacation Rentals website will be to provide information to visitors about different lake house rental properties that are available through our company. The site will tell the story of who we are as a company and give visitors the necessary tools to contact us, research common questions and to book any available property. Past customer reviews will also be displayed to give users added comfort in using the site to book their next vacation.

Audience

Our target audience will be couples or families looking for information on an affordable, fun, accessible vacation property by the lake. Our visitors may or may not be technologically savvy so the site should be easy to navigate and understand. Graphical elements should be fun and appealing to all ages.
